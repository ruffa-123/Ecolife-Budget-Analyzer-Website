Compare conventional vs sustainable urban living — cost, emissions, and annual impact

EcoLife Budget Analyzer lets you estimate monthly living costs for conventional choices (car commutes, standard appliances, convenience shopping) and compare them with sustainable alternatives (public transit, energy-efficient homes, local/seasonal food). It also provides a simulated annual emissions estimate and shows the yearly cost difference so households and planners can make informed, sustainable decisions aligned with SDG 11.

Use the Planner to create scenarios (e.g., "Car → Transit", "Standard AC → Inverter AC") and see both financial and environmental outcomes.

SDG 11 — Sustainable Cities & Communities: what EcoLife does

EcoLife aligns budgeting recommendations with SDG11 priorities — affordable housing, low-carbon mobility, resilient infrastructure, and reduced consumption waste. The analyzer translates daily lifestyle choices into monthly cost figures and simulated annual emissions so users can compare conventional vs sustainable scenarios side-by-side.

Cost comparison

See monthly and annual cost differences between conventional and sustainable options — transport, housing, energy, and groceries.

Simulated emissions

Get a simple, transparent estimate of annual emissions for each scenario (tonnes CO₂e) to guide low-carbon choices.

Annual impact & savings

View year-over-year cost savings and the combined financial + environmental benefit of switching to sustainable options.
